# Pro Account Guide

<div class="plan-badges"><span class="plan-badge plan-pro">Pro</span></div>

Pro accounts add advanced setup options, terminology support, online post-editing, and export workflows for CAT tools.

## Recommended Workflow

1. [Start a new quality check](../help-center/running-a-quality-check/start-new-quality-check.md).
2. Add Pro setup options as needed:
   - [Filter segments by Locked status](../help-center/pro-features/filter-locked-status.md)
   - [Filter segments by Percent range](../help-center/pro-features/filter-percent-range.md)
   - [Upload and validate a termbase](../help-center/pro-features/upload-validate-termbase.md)
   - [Check source and target inconsistencies](../help-center/pro-features/check-inconsistencies.md)
   - [Choose the AI response language](../help-center/pro-features/ai-response-language.md)
3. [Open the online post-editing workspace](../help-center/online-post-editing/open-workspace.md).
4. [Edit target text online](../help-center/online-post-editing/edit-target-text.md).
5. [Propagate repeated-segment changes](../help-center/online-post-editing/propagate-changes.md).
6. [Export corrected XLIFF files](../help-center/online-post-editing/export-corrected-xliff.md) or [download annotated XLIFF files](../help-center/cat-tool-integrations/download-annotated-xliff.md).

## Manage Pro

Use [Manage your Pro subscription](../help-center/pro-features/manage-pro-subscription.md) to review or change your active Pro plan.
