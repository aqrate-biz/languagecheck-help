# Team Account Guide

<div class="plan-badges"><span class="plan-badge plan-team">Team</span></div>

Team accounts are built around shared words, roles, team check visibility, and collaboration on individual checks.

## Recommended Workflow

1. [Join a team invitation](../help-center/account-and-teams/join-team-invitation.md), if you have been invited.
2. [Understand team roles and permissions](../help-center/account-and-teams/team-roles-permissions.md).
3. Owners can [manage team members](../help-center/account-and-teams/manage-team-members.md).
4. Owners and Business Managers can [buy words for a team wallet](../help-center/account-and-teams/buy-team-words.md).
5. Team members can [use shared team words](../help-center/account-and-teams/use-shared-team-words.md) to run checks.
6. Roles with permission can [view team checks](../help-center/account-and-teams/view-team-checks.md).
7. Use [external collaborators](../help-center/account-and-teams/invite-external-collaborators.md) for check-specific review and post-editing access.

## Important Notes

When a user accepts a team invitation, their available personal words may be transferred to the team wallet.
