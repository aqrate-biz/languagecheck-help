<div class="plan-badges"><span class="plan-badge plan-team">Team</span></div>

## What is a team wallet?

A team wallet is a shared pool of words used by team members to run checks.

## Who can buy team words?

Owners and Business Managers can buy words for the team wallet.

## Can I invite someone outside the team?

Yes. You can invite an external collaborator to a single check if your role has permission.
