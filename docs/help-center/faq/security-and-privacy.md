## Are my files used to train AI models?

No. Uploaded files are used for your checks and are not used to train AI models.

## Can I delete uploaded files?

Yes. You can delete checks. Files are automatically deleted after 30 days if you do not delete them earlier.

## Where is my data stored?

You choose EU or US storage during account setup.
