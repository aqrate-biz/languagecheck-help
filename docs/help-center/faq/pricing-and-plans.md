<div class="plan-badges"><span class="plan-badge plan-normal">Standard</span><span class="plan-badge plan-pro">Pro</span><span class="plan-badge plan-team">Team</span></div>

## How does pricing work?

LanguageCheck uses word-based pricing. You can buy word bundles and use words to run checks.

## What is included in Pro?

Pro includes filters, termbases, inconsistency checks, target length checks, response language selection, online post-editing, and XLIFF export features.

## Can teams buy shared words?

Yes. Team roles with permission can buy words for the shared team wallet.
