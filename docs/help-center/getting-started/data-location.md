# Choose Where Your Uploaded Data Is Stored

<div class="plan-badges"><span class="plan-badge plan-normal">Standard</span><span class="plan-badge plan-pro">Pro</span><span class="plan-badge plan-team">Team</span></div>

LanguageCheck asks where uploaded data should be stored during account setup.

## Steps

1. Choose **European Union (EU)** or **United States (US)**.
2. Review the pricing and invoice currency shown for that location.
3. Read the Terms of Use and Privacy Policy shown during setup.
4. Accept the required conditions and continue.

## Notes and Limitations

Uploaded files are used only for your checks and are not used to train AI models. The selected store also affects the currency used for future purchases. If you need to change store later, contact support.
