# When Termbase Adds Words to a Check

<div class="plan-badges"><span class="plan-badge plan-pro">Pro</span><span class="plan-badge plan-team">Team</span></div>

The check summary may show additional words when a termbase is applied.

## Why It Happens

LanguageCheck imports and processes the termbase so the AI can compare terminology against the uploaded segments.

## What to Check

1. Review the total word count.
2. Confirm that the termbase is correct.
3. Make sure your wallet has enough words.
4. Send the check only after reviewing the summary.
