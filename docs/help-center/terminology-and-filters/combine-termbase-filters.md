# Combine Termbase Checks with Segment Filters

<div class="plan-badges"><span class="plan-badge plan-pro">Pro</span><span class="plan-badge plan-team">Team</span></div>

Use filters and a termbase together when only part of a project needs terminology review.

## Steps

1. Upload the bilingual file(s).
2. Configure Locked or Percent filters.
3. Upload and validate the termbase.
4. Review all Pro options in the check summary.
5. Send the check.

## Notes and Limitations

Filters determine which segments are analyzed. Termbase words are also added to the word count.
