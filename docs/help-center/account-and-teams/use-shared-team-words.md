# Use Shared Team Words

<div class="plan-badges"><span class="plan-badge plan-team">Team</span></div>

Team checks consume words from the team wallet.

## Steps

1. Join the team invitation.
2. Confirm the word counter shows the team wallet.
3. Start a new check.
4. Review the summary and send the check if the team wallet has enough words.

## Notes and Limitations

If the team wallet does not have enough words, contact a team administrator or request words if the app shows that option.
