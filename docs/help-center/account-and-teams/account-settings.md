# Update Your Name and Account Settings

<div class="plan-badges"><span class="plan-badge plan-normal">Standard</span><span class="plan-badge plan-pro">Pro</span><span class="plan-badge plan-team">Team</span></div>

Use Settings to update your displayed name, review the authentication method, manage your password, and set Pro propagation options.

## Steps

1. Open the user menu.
2. Select **Settings**.
3. Update **Name** in **User info**.
4. Review whether you signed up with Google or email/password.
5. Use **Change password** or **Set a password** if needed.

## Notes and Limitations

Uploaded data location is shown in settings but is not editable.
