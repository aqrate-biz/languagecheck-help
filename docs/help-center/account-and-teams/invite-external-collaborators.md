# Invite External Collaborators to a Check

<div class="plan-badges"><span class="plan-badge plan-team">Team</span></div>

Invite an external collaborator when someone needs access to one check for review or post-editing.

## Steps

1. Open a completed check.
2. In **Collaborate**, select **Invite collaborator**.
3. Enter the collaborator email address.
4. Send the invitation.

## Notes and Limitations

Collaborators can view check details and post-edit the invited check. They do not become full team members.
