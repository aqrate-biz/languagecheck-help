# Contact Support to Change Marketing Preferences or Delete an Account

<div class="plan-badges"><span class="plan-badge plan-normal">Normal</span><span class="plan-badge plan-pro">Pro</span><span class="plan-badge plan-team">Team</span></div>

Some account changes require support assistance.

## What Requires Support

- Changing your agreement to receive updates and promotional news.
- Deleting your account.
- Changing account store or country after setup.

## Steps

1. Email `crew@languagecheck.ai`.
2. Include the account email.
3. Explain the change you need.
