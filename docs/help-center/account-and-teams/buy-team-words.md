# Buy Words for a Team Wallet

<div class="plan-badges"><span class="plan-badge plan-team">Team</span></div>

Team words are purchased into the shared team wallet.

## Steps

1. Open **Manage your team** or the user menu.
2. Select **Add words to team wallet** or **Buy more words**.
3. Choose a word bundle.
4. Complete checkout.

## Notes and Limitations

Only Owners and Business Managers can buy words for the team wallet.
