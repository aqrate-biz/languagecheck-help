# Billing Requirements for Businesses and Private Users

<div class="plan-badges"><span class="plan-badge plan-normal">Normal</span><span class="plan-badge plan-pro">Pro</span><span class="plan-badge plan-team">Team</span></div>

Billing fields depend on whether you purchase as a business or private individual.

## Requirements

- Businesses must provide VAT or Tax ID information when required.
- Italian businesses must provide SDI Code or PEC.
- Italian private individuals may need Codice Fiscale.
- Address, city, postal code, and country are required.

## Notes and Limitations

Invalid VAT or tax information can prevent checkout from continuing.
