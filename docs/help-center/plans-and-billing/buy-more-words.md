# Buy More Words

<div class="plan-badges"><span class="plan-badge plan-normal">Standard</span><span class="plan-badge plan-pro">Pro</span><span class="plan-badge plan-team">Team</span></div>

Buy word bundles when your wallet does not have enough words for a check.

## Steps

1. Open the user menu.
2. Select **Buy more words**.
3. Choose a bundle.
4. Complete Stripe checkout.
5. Return to LanguageCheck to see your updated wallet balance.

## Notes and Limitations

In a team, only roles with permission to buy words can buy words for the team wallet.
