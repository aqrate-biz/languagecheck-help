# Understand Words and Word Wallets

<div class="plan-badges"><span class="plan-badge plan-normal">Standard</span><span class="plan-badge plan-pro">Pro</span><span class="plan-badge plan-team">Team</span></div>

Words are the credit units used to run quality checks.

## How It Works

- Personal accounts use a personal word wallet.
- Team accounts use the team word wallet.
- Running a check consumes words based on the analyzed content.
- The word counter in the header shows available words.

## Notes and Limitations

Team members may not be allowed to buy words, depending on their role.
