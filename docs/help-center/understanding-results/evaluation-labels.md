# Understand Evaluation Labels

<div class="plan-badges"><span class="plan-badge plan-normal">Normal</span><span class="plan-badge plan-pro">Pro</span><span class="plan-badge plan-team">Team</span></div>

Evaluation labels help you prioritize human review.

## Labels

- **Flawless**: no relevant problem detected.
- **Needs Improvement**: the translation is usable but may need correction.
- **Incorrect Meaning**: the target may misrepresent the source and should be reviewed with priority.

## Notes and Limitations

Labels are AI-assisted quality signals, not final linguistic judgments.
