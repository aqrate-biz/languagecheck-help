# Read the Result Summary Chart

<div class="plan-badges"><span class="plan-badge plan-normal">Standard</span><span class="plan-badge plan-pro">Pro</span><span class="plan-badge plan-team">Team</span></div>

The result summary chart groups checked segments into practical evaluation categories. Use the chart to understand where most of the review work is concentrated.

## How to Read It

1. **Flawless** means no issues were detected and the translation is clear and accurate.
2. **Needs Improvement** means the translation is generally understandable but contains errors, such as linguistic mistakes, deviations from the termbase, or stylistic issues.
3. **Incorrect Meaning** means the translation is mostly wrong: the meaning may be inaccurate, different from the source, incomplete, or even reversed.

## Notes and Limitations

AI results are designed to guide human review. Always make the final decision yourself.
