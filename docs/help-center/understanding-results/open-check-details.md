# Open Check Details

<div class="plan-badges"><span class="plan-badge plan-normal">Standard</span><span class="plan-badge plan-pro">Pro</span><span class="plan-badge plan-team">Team</span></div>

Open check details to monitor progress, access the results, and download files.

## Steps

1. Select **Check history**.
2. Open the check you want to review.
3. Review the results. Download the report, get an annotated XLIFF, or access the online post-editing workspace.

## Notes and Limitations

Depending on their role, team members may see checks created by others.
